RELATIONSHIP SUMMARY:
====================

1. USERS (1) ──────── (1) EMPLOYEES
   - One user account per employee
   - Authentication and authorization

2. EMPLOYEES (N) ──── (1) DEPARTMENTS
   - Many employees belong to one department
   - Department assignment

3. EMPLOYEES (N) ──── (1) POSITIONS
   - Many employees can have same position
   - Job role definition

4. EMPLOYEES (N) ──── (1) EMPLOYEES (Manager)
   - Self-referencing relationship
   - Manager-subordinate hierarchy

5. EMPLOYEES (1) ──── (N) ATTENDANCE_RECORDS
   - One employee has many attendance records
   - Daily attendance tracking

6. EMPLOYEES (1) ──── (N) TIME_OFF_REQUESTS
   - One employee can make many requests
   - Leave management

7. EMPLOYEES (1) ──── (N) PERFORMANCE_REVIEWS
   - One employee has many reviews
   - Performance tracking

8. EMPLOYEES (1) ──── (N) SALARY_HISTORY
   - One employee has salary history
   - Compensation tracking

9. EMPLOYEES (1) ──── (N) EMERGENCY_CONTACTS
   - One employee has multiple contacts
   - Emergency information

10. EMPLOYEES (1) ──── (N) DOCUMENTS
    - One employee has many documents
    - File management

11. USERS (1) ──────── (N) NOTIFICATIONS
    - One user receives many notifications
    - System messaging

12. USERS (1) ──────── (N) AUDIT_LOGS
    - One user generates many log entries
    - Activity tracking

CARDINALITY NOTATION:
=====================
- (1) = One
- (N) = Many
- (FK) = Foreign Key
- (PK) = Primary Key
- ◄─── = Relationship direction
